About Guru Nanak Institute of Technology

Guru Nanak Institute of Technology was instituted with a vision to empower the aspiring professionals with the technological knowledge and professional expertise under the aegis of JIS Group Educational Initiatives in the year 2003. This technical campus offers undergraduate and post graduate courses under West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT now MAKAUT) and some courses are NBA Accredited. The Institute is approved by AICTE and accredited by UGC, NAAC and located in one of the prime locations in North Kolkata near Sodepur. The Institute offers latest technology oriented courses as per industry trends and nurtures creativity, innovations and research initiatives both at faculty and student levels to enrich and enhance the teaching learning process. This approach equips the students to keep pace with the dynamic evolution and changing demands of science and technology both in academics and industry. The trend-setting academic endeavors of GNIT have produced some of the finest scholars and ace industry professionals. The college alumni are working creditably ell and have brought fame and laurels for both the institute and themselves.

Eligibiity Criteria

Anybody can participate in the hackthon from both academia and industry.


There must be a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members, from same college or different college or maybe from the industry.


The decision of the jury shall be final and binding.


The submissions will be judged on the basis of problem analysis, creativity/innovation, feasibility, detailing, scalability and originality.
